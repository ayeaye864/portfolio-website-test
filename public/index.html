About

PROJECT AYA is a freelance coder who specialises in website building, UX design and research. Her other works include 3D modelling and creating game experiences in Unreal Engine as well as generative art through algorithms.

As a hobby, she indulges in photography of her surroundings and creating videos of her experiences. She can also be found creating stories and interactive fiction. Her works focus on encapsulating her creative mind and inspiring growth and ambition.
